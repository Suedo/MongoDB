Tools:

1. mongostat: shows incoming operations in real time
2. mongotop: shows which collection we are reading from and writting to
3. mongoreplay: monitor, record and replay network traffic
4. mongo: admin shell
5. profiler: logs queries for us
6. compass: GUI to look at current state of DB, plus do real time analysis
7. mtools: public github repo to spin up replica sets and sharded clusters locally for testing, and load testing
